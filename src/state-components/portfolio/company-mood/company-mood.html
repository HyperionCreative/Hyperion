I. Getting started

We started this redesign project like most of our other projects, by getting to know the client, their business and the problems they are looking to solve. In this case, their goals were to redesign their website with a more professional and trustworthy look, all while restructuring it to better explains all the advantages their product can offer.

To achieve this, we started with a series of conferences and questions.

II. Defining the structure

Once we had a clear picture of Company Mood’s message, we started exploring ways in which this information could be communicated to users and eventually defined the site’s structure.
